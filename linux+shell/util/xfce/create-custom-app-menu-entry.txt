Make a .desktop file.

[Desktop Entry]
Version=1.0
Type=Application
Name=IDEA
Exec=/home/user/home/app/idea/bin/idea.sh
Icon=/home/user/home/app/idea/bin/idea.svg
Categories=Development


Place it in:
/usr/share/applications  (affects all users)
[your-home]/.local/share/applications (affects chosen user)
